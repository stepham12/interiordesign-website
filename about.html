EURIMAGE SDN BHD

Incorporate in Malaysia

Eurimage is an established name in the Malaysian construction industry that offers a full range of commercial and office interior solutions and services to clients in the corporate, retail, commercial and government sectors.

With over 20 years in the business, Eurimage continues to grow from strength to strength by providing clients with trusted and quality services in the most effective and efficient manner.

Our core competencies include:

  • Interior design fit-out works
  • Interior architectural works
  • Joinery works
  • Refurbishment and modernization
  • Interior design and Space planning

Started out in 1992 as a small scale renovator, Eurimage has since modernized many key landmarks in Malaysia by converting empty shell spaces into affluent offices, sophisticated retail outlets and luxurious multi-level homes. From floor, wall to ceiling finishes, glass and metal works, to sanitary and decorative fittings, Eurimage have always been recognized for their superior services in interior architectural and fit out works.

In line with providing end-to-end services, Eurimage provides strong support in joinery, mechanical and electrical, interior designing and space planning giving clients a one-stop solution for their construction needs.

Eurimage has gained significant industry recognition over the years by undertaking some remarkable projects from renowned clients. Some of the key projects from their extensive portfolio include Parkson chain retail stores, Lion group office, One Residency service apartments, Lanson Place service apartments, Kluang hospital and Nu Sentral shopping mall.

One leading advantage of Eurimage is the ability to help clients make the most of their building assets by transforming simple spaces into modern, functional areas for working, shopping, entertainment or living thereby increasing property value.

Throughout the years, Eurimage has remained faithful in upholding three(3) prominent values in their daily operations - adhering to health and safety requirements, provision of quality materials and timely deliverance of project handover.

To date, Eurimage possesses an impressive portfolio of having completed more than 260 prominent projects in Malaysia.
